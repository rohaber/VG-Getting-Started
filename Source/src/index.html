VideoGuard Everywhere DRM

Content is still king, in the world of video. As consumers worldwide find more ways to consume video content and the Internet of Everything influences their lifestyle, “Anytime, anywhere, any device” is clearly the new normal.

A unified content-protection solution, Cisco VideoGuard Everywhere DRM wholly addresses the issues of capability, compatibility, and responsibility.

Features

A modern effective content-protection solution must take a holistic approach. It should address below three critical considerations:


Capabilities

Capabilities that help you evolve quickly to deliver an extensive feature set in a dynamic market.

  • Event-Based Entitlements
  • Multi-DRM: Manage Devices with Different DRMs
  • Provide a Variety of Concurrency Limits
  • Policy, Location, and CDN Access Control
  • Proximity Control
  • Transparent Home Networking Experience
  • DTCP-IP Import and Export
  • CA Termination
  • Emergency Alerts on Live Channels

Compatibility

Compatibility that lets you deliver multiple services over multiple networks, in a multi-DRM deployment that is widely approved by content owners.

  • 20+ Devices Support
  • Content formats
  • Encoders
  • CDNs
  • Interoperability standards
  • Virtualization and cloud
  • User experience and application development

Responsibility

Responsibility that provides you with ongoing expertise and support.

  • Content Protection
  • Service Protection

Supported Devices

Our solution is compatible with the most popular managed and unmanaged devices - including PCs, Macs, tablets, mobile phones, STBs, game consoles, and smart TVs - so you can enforce similar business rules across multiple platforms.
